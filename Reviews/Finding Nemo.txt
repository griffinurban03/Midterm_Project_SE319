The animation is stunning, bringing the underwater world to life with vibrant colors and intricate details. The film beautifully explores themes of parental love, bravery, and the importance of letting go. The bond between Marlin and Dory, a forgetful blue tang voiced by Ellen DeGeneres, adds humor and heart to the story.
